Sumnmative - The Ball

Here is my summative game. Follow the in-game instructions.

My code:

  • -Uses if/else statements to make levels - learned from U4L11 decision structures
  • -Applies knowledge to make shapes move - learned from "using the keyboard" and "using the mouse"
  • -Applies knowledge on using images and sounds from U4L10
  • -Makes extensive use of variables and math to control them.
  • -Detects collisions between obstacles and the ball.
  • -Detects when the ball is clicked using a mouse press check when the two are touching.
Previously, I looked at Ms. Edward's lesson-website using the "Inspect Element" tool to see how she embedded sketch.js, created text, etc. I used this knowledge for this most index.html files in the Processing unit.
