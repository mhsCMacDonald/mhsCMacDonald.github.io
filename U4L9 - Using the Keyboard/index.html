U4L9 - Using the Keyboard

I have created a simple 2 player game. The objective is to reach the side guarded by your opponent. Strafe back and forth to confuse your opponent and to get a breakaway.

My code:

  • Allows 2 players to accelerate with the WASD/arrow keys
  • Stops the game and displays text if a player has won, or restart the game if the mouse is clicked
  • Allows the players to bounce off of each other with collideRectRect
  • Allows the players to bounce off of walls
  • Simultes friction by reducing speed over time
  • Prevets players from escaping the playable area by forcing them back in
I also took a look at Ms. Edward's lesson-website using the "Inspect Element" tool to see how she embedded sketch.js, created text, etc. I used this knowledge for this index.html file.
